Charlotte's Web（夏洛特的网）—— 一只猪的故事。几年前陪小妹买书时翻过，最近看到电影就看了下，值得一看。中文字幕多少影响观感。

台词摘录

As summer ended, so did the excitement about the web.

Charlotte needed to think of something special — special enough to change the way people saw the world, or at least one pig in the world. The truth is, Charlotte feared she would never find a word that could do all that.
